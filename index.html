    FAQ

    How does Sinch work?

    While signing up you're given the option of basic, or premium use of Sinch. The basic plan will not charge you monthly, but will charge $9.99 per use for creating your date plans and/or gift ideas. The premium service gives you 4 complimentary plans each month, customized suggestions for birthday, anniversary, or other special occasions, exclusive date and gift options from local venues and businesses, and direct contact with an experience specialist to help you with booking and planning your date.

    What type of exclusive date and gift options will you offer?

    These exclusive offers will include experiences like a guided couples tour of the National Museum of African American History & Culture in D.C., a customized dining experience at a local restaurant with a table adorned especially for you and your guest, or a custom box of baked goods embellished with a unique design and a love note attached.

    What is included in my date plans?

    The assigned experience specialist will give you a set of 3 plans to choose from for your selected time frame, mood, and budget. They'll also give you tips on how to make the experience memorable and unique for you and your partner.

    How long does it take to get my plans back?

    It will take up to 24 hours to receive your plans from your assigned experience specialist. Good things take time, and you're still saving time by having us do the planning for you ; )

    What if I don't like my experience?

    The experience specialist will send you another round of plans. If that still doesn't meet your expectations, we'll refund you the cost of your date but will require that you provide us feedback of what went wrong so that we can improve the experience moving forward.

    As a small business or investor, how can I partner with you?

    Business owners, please email us with your proposed partnership to take advantage of the users on our platform that are looking for great date options or romantic gifts. Investors, email us with the details of your interest.
